About Me

Hello, my name is Turboflower. I’m a digital artist with a passion for creating Japanese styled drawings, specializing in illustration and character design.

I began my art journey in March 2020 and have been dedicated to developing my skills and creativity ever since. Over the years, I’ve explored various styles and techniques, building a strong foundation in digital art and continually pushing my artistic boundaries

Constantly driven to enhance my craft, I enjoy exploring new techniques and experimenting with diverse art forms. From web design to 3D art, I’m always expanding my skill set to bring new perspectives to my work.
